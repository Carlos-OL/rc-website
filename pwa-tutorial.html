Save the App to Your Home Screen

Install the website as a PWA (Progressive Web App) to receive push notifications on your phone — even when your browser isn't open.

First — log in on your mobile browser
①
On your phone, go to restockbotalerts.com and tap Customer Portal at the top of the page
→
②
Enter your email to log in — you'll receive a magic link to your inbox
→
③
Tap the magic link on your phone — it will open the dashboard in a new browser tab. Now save it from there.
🍎
iPhone / iPad
Safari or Chrome
  1. 1
    With the dashboard open in your browser, tap the Share button
    It's the box-with-arrow icon at the bottom of the screen (or top on iPad).
  2. 2
    Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
    Give it a name if you'd like, then tap Add in the top right.
  3. 3
    Open the app from your home screen icon
    Important: always launch from the icon — not from the browser.
  4. 4
    Go to Account → Settings and enable push notifications
    You're all set. Alerts will now arrive like native app notifications.
🤖
Android
Chrome browser required
  1. 1
    With the dashboard open in Chrome, tap the ⋮ menu in the top right
    This is the three-dot menu at the top right of the Chrome browser.
  2. 2
    Tap Add to Home Screen or Install App
    The exact label may vary slightly by Android version — both do the same thing.
  3. 3
    Tap Install to confirm
    The app icon will appear on your home screen immediately.
  4. 4
    Open from the icon, then enable push notifications in Account → Settings
    Always launch from the home screen icon — not from Chrome.
⚠️
Important — Enable Notifications From the App, Not the Browser
Once the icon is on your home screen, always open the site from that icon — not from Safari or Chrome. Push notifications are only active when you're running the PWA. After opening it from the icon, go to Account → Settings → Push Notifications and enable the alerts you want. This step must be done from within the PWA.
Push notification settings are per device — repeat these steps on each device where you want alerts.
All other settings (cashback %, Pure tier, buyer visibility) sync automatically across all your devices.
